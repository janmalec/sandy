sandy.sampling module¶

Created on Mon Mar 19 22:51:03 2018

@author: Luca Fiorito

sandy.sampling.sampling(iargs=None)¶

Construct multivariate normal distributions with a unit vector for mean and with relative covariances taken from the evaluated files. Perturbation factors are sampled with the same multigroup structure of the covariance matrix, and are applied to the pointwise data to produce the perturbed files.
